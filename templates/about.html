About us

Our Mission

Our mission is to teach you how to heal yourself.

Our Aim

Our aim is to make Reiki accessible to everyone.

  1. Make treatments easy to access anytime, anywhere.
  2. Provide clear, easy-to-understand Reiki training for self-healing.
  3. Demystify the technique.

Hi. I’m Adele.

I’m an intuitive healer and an empath. I learned to meditate and became a yogi to overcome a chronic illness and life-long anxiety. This led to a change in career from neuroscience researcher to yoga and meditation teacher. I first learned Reiki to help manage my own energy and that of my family, and this has expanded to include my community. For me, Reiki was a natural development as a daily meditation and mindfulness practice and melded perfectly with yogic philosophy. My extensive passion for natural healing modalities has led me to self-study and live the principles of Traditional Chinese Medicine and Ayurveda, and to seek formal training and mentoring in shamanic healing practices and meridian-based yoga therapy. When I’m treating clients, I love seeing people connect to their own inner spirit and source of personal healing power. I see what the spirit needs and assist the healing process with love and understanding. I serve the highest good of each person — to be heard, cared for and supported in a loving and safe environment.
