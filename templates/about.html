Our Mission

Hi. I’m Adele.

I became an energy healer via the unusual route of a PhD and working as a neuroscientist for many years. I’ve always been fascinated with science and how the world and our bodies work. During a chronic state of unwellness while my boys were young, I learned to meditate and retrained as a yoga and meditation teacher. It was a natural extension of my curiosity about how things work - only this time my own body and mind were under the ‘microscope’ of my developing conscious awareness. Instead of the fluorescent light scanning microscopy I’d left behind in the lab, it was light scanning healing meditations and the like that fixed me up and expanded my mind to connect to universal consciousness. Reiki is the fastest and most efficient way I’ve learned so far to tap into this state. It’s like hot-wiring the body to be able to receive the energy that’s all around us all the time. The practitioner acts as a conduit for this energy by using focussed attention and positive intention. The healing is done by your own body - it simply puts the extra boost of energy to good use. When I’m treating people, I love seeing the shifts that start to happen as they reconnect to their own inner guidance. It shines out of their eyes. That’s the best bit. xxx
